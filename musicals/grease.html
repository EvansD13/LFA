About the Musical

Grease is a 1971 musical by Jim Jacobs and Warren Casey. The musical is named for the 1950s United States working-class youth subculture known as greasers.

Characters include

Danny Zuko

Leader of the T-Birds

Sandy Olsson

New girl in town

  • "Hopelessly Devoted to You"
