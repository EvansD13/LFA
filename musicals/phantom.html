About the Musical

The Phantom of the Opera is a musical with music by Andrew Lloyd Webber and lyrics by Charles Hart. It is based on the French novel Le Fantôme de l'Opéra by Gaston Leroux.

Characters include

The Phantom

A mysterious man who lives beneath the Paris Opera House

Christine Daaé

A young soprano who becomes the object of the Phantom's affections

Songs include

  • "The Phantom of the Opera"
  • "All I Ask of You"
  • "Music of the Night"
