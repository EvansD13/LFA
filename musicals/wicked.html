About the Musical

Wicked is a Broadway musical with music and lyrics by Stephen Schwartz and book by Winnie Holzman. It is based on the 1995 Gregory Maguire novel Wicked: The Life and Times of the Wicked Witch of the West.

Characters include

Elphaba

The Wicked Witch of the West

Glinda

The Good Witch

Songs include

  • "Defying Gravity"
  • "Popular"
  • "For Good"
